projects

Project: Design website Webmyday

At the top, a video of the office is shown to give the viewer a feeling for the company's atmosphere, along with a company description and a call to action to contact them. Then, the different work fields are displayed with buttons that link to a detailed page for each one. Lastly, it shows companies that have already had a positive experience working with this company.

The homepage shows several images of the office to introduce customers to the atmosphere of the company. It also highlights the goals this company can help clients achieve and explains the company's way of working.

At the bottom of the homepage, there is a section showing how many years the company has been active and ready to help customers. It also includes a call to action to contact them and a footer with links to other pages, location information, and contact details for Webmyday.

process

Style guide with easy-to-read fonts to maintain a professional atmosphere. Neutral colors create a clean and formal look. A playful subtitle color shows that the company is human-centered and not overly formal or business-like.

The colors at the top of this page are the primary colors: black, gray, and blue, which create a professional and trustworthy atmosphere. The secondary colors below are playful pastel tones that show the company is approachable and not an overly formal business that only communicates in jargon.

Project: Design website Havik Invest

A large image of the area where the company operates creates a strong first impression. White space is used throughout the design to maintain a professional and clean appearance. The primary colors are taken directly from the logo to stay consistent with the company's branding.

Services are displayed in alternating colors to keep the design visually engaging. A white background behind the service descriptions creates contrast and gives the page a lively appearance. The light-colored footer includes a cutout for the logo to improve visibility and create contrast with the service section above it.

The service detail page includes a fitting image with a blue overlay to improve title visibility and add depth, making the page feel less flat. It contains information about the specific service along with a call to action for quick conversions. A gray section highlights other available services, adding contrast to the section above while keeping the service blocks visually consistent with the homepage. The footer follows the same style as the homepage and uses a light color to create contrast with the gray section above it.

process

Style guide with professional titles and fonts that match the visual style of the logo. Natural colors create a clean appearance, while the combination of blue and orange-brown tones provides complementary contrast. Square-shaped buttons match the sharp shapes used in the logo.

about me

I am a creative UI/UX designer. I studied Communication and Multimedia Design. During my studies, I worked within the four design areas: Human, Tech, Void, and Story.

Human focuses on user research and testing, combined with psychology, to identify the needs of the customer.

Tech is mainly focused on coding and building using tools and technologies such as Arduino, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Void is about designing for the future and predicting upcoming trends by researching developments from the past.

Story focuses on storytelling through graphic design using tools such as photography, illustration, and video.

I take my work seriously and am driven to find the right solution to meet the customer's needs. I do have to warn you about my dry sense of humor though :). My passions are graphic design, storytelling, and music.
