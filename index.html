projects

Project: Design Concept website Webmyday

Challenge
Webmyday needed a homepage that would immediately communicate professionalism while remaining approachable to potential clients. Visitors should quickly understand what the company does and feel encouraged to get in touch.

Approach
The focus was placed on creating a clear visual hierarchy and presenting the company culture in an authentic way. User attention was guided towards the most important information through strategic content placement and strong calls to action.

Solution
A hero video was positioned above the fold to showcase the company’s working environment and create an engaging first impression. Service highlights and clear contact opportunities were added to help visitors quickly navigate towards relevant information and conversion points.

Challenge
Potential clients often need reassurance before contacting a digital agency. Building trust was therefore an important objective within the homepage design.

Approach
The design uses authentic office photography, concise messaging and generous whitespace to create a transparent and professional user experience.

Solution
The layout separates information into clear content blocks, making the page easy to scan while maintaining visual balance. This helps users understand the company’s process and expertise without feeling overwhelmed by information.

The final section reinforces credibility by highlighting the company's experience and achievements. A strong call to action motivates visitors to make contact, while the footer provides easy access to important company information and navigation links.

process

The style guide was designed to balance professionalism with approachability. Clean typography improves readability, while a combination of neutral and accent colors creates a trustworthy visual identity that reflects the company's values.

The primary color palette uses dark blue, gray and black tones to communicate reliability and expertise. Supporting pastel colors add personality and create a more human-centered brand experience without compromising professionalism.

Project: Design Concept website Havik Invest

Challenge
Havik Invest required a website that would communicate reliability and professionalism while presenting its services in a clear and accessible way.

Approach
The homepage was designed around a strong visual structure, combining impactful imagery with a clear content hierarchy to guide visitors through the page.

Solution
Large visual elements establish an immediate professional impression, while consistent use of brand colors strengthens recognition. Generous whitespace improves readability and supports a premium visual experience.

Challenge
The website contains multiple service offerings that needed to be presented without overwhelming visitors.

Approach
A structured layout and clear separation between content sections were used to improve navigation and information discovery.

Solution
Alternating background colors help distinguish sections and maintain visual engagement throughout the page. A clear hierarchy allows users to quickly understand available services while preserving a clean and professional aesthetic.

Challenge
The service pages needed to provide detailed information while encouraging users to take action.

Approach
The focus was placed on balancing informative content with conversion-oriented design principles.

Solution
Consistent visual elements create a seamless experience between homepage and service pages. Clear calls to action support lead generation, while structured content helps visitors understand services efficiently and confidently.

process

The visual identity was developed around the existing logo and brand values. Professional typography, natural color tones and structured layouts create a reliable appearance that aligns with the expectations of the target audience.
